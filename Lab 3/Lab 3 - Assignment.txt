Assignment 3: Connecting a Display to RPi to read data from the DHT11 sensor

Part 4:
In this Assignment, we want to use a Display to show the temperature or humidity once it is recorded by RPi. Connect the LCD DISPLAY board, which is provided to each group, on the RPi GPIOs. Develop a C or Python routine to show the RPi transaction data on the display. Details about the LCD Display are provided in separate. Note, Assignment 3 assumes that you have completed all relevant parts of Assignments 1 and 2.
